About twitto.be

Twitto.be is a directory of Belgian twitter users.

Features:

  • Explore Belgian twitter influencers by topic, keyword, location and language
  • Visualize statistics about Belgian twitter users

Some key figures:

  • More than 120000 Belgian twitter users indexed
  • 13000+ people have visited twitto.be since June 2013
  • Record visits peaks of more than 2500 visitors in a day
